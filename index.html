Eddie Stern Project

This was the Masterclass Workshop held by NSHM Knowledge Campus, Kolkata in the year 2019 in December where the Internationally acclaimed yogi Eddie stern from New York came to campus to teach the art of pranayama & meditation. I designed the collateralls such as Posters, Mailers, Leaflets, Certificate, Invites, banners, Standee, Backdrops, Dropdown and also made many social media videos to promote the event.
